Air Fryer Meatloaf

Description

Here’s a brief overview of what you can expect when you make this air fryer meatloaf: Mix the meat, onion, egg, breadcrumbs, and seasonings in a bowl. Transfer the mixture to small loaf pan, press in the mushrooms, and coat with oil. Roast until browned, let cool slightly, the cut into slices.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can fat-free condensed cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder, or to taste
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    Sauté ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until browned and crumbly; 5 to 10 minutes.
